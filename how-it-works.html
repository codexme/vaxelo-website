How It Works

From market to signal
in 10 seconds.

No setup. No guessing. Open any Kalshi market and Vaxelo fires 4 AI agents simultaneously to give you one clear signal.

The 4-Step Process

Simple to use.
Powerful under the hood.

01

Install the Extension

One click from Chrome Web Store. Enter your free Groq API key in the Setup tab. You are ready in under 2 minutes.

What You Need

  • Google Chrome browser
  • Free Groq API key from console.groq.com
  • A Kalshi account to place trades
  • Optional: Lemon Squeezy license for unlimited signals
02

Open Any Kalshi Market

Navigate to any prediction market on Kalshi. The Vaxelo panel appears automatically in the corner of the page — no clicking required.

Supported Markets

  • Crypto markets (BTC, ETH, price predictions)
  • Sports markets (game outcomes, scores)
  • Politics markets (elections, policy)
  • Economics markets (rates, GDP, CPI)
  • Any market on Kalshi's platform
03

4 AI Brains Fire in Parallel

Reader, Spy, Researcher, and Judge all run simultaneously using Promise.all(). No sequential waiting. Full analysis in under 10 seconds.

What Each Brain Does

  • Reader: Scrapes the full Kalshi page in real time
  • Spy: Compares Kalshi vs Polymarket for price gaps
  • Researcher: Hits 15 external sources simultaneously
  • Judge: Weighs all inputs and issues final verdict
04

Get Your Signal

BUY YES, BUY NO, or HOLD — with probability estimate, expected value calculation, and Kelly-optimal position size. You make the final call.

What You See

  • Signal: BUY YES / BUY NO / HOLD
  • Probability: AI-calculated percentage
  • Expected Value: Is this bet positive EV?
  • Kelly Size: How much of your bankroll to risk
  • Confidence level: How much the 4 brains agree

FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need a Groq API key? +
Yes. Groq provides the AI reasoning layer for Vaxelo. Their API is free to use — go to console.groq.com, create a free account, and generate an API key. It takes under 2 minutes. Your key is stored only on your device and never sent to Vaxelo servers.
Does Vaxelo work on mobile? +
Vaxelo is currently a Chrome extension and works on desktop Chrome only. A mobile app is planned for V2.0. In the meantime, use it on any laptop or desktop running Chrome.
How accurate is the signal? +
Our target accuracy is 70%. Prediction markets are inherently probabilistic — even the best models are not 100% accurate. Vaxelo is designed to find positive expected value opportunities, not to win every single trade. Always treat signals as one input among many and never risk more than you can afford to lose.
Is this financial advice? +
No. Vaxelo AI is an educational and analytical tool only. Nothing produced by Vaxelo constitutes financial advice. We are not registered financial advisors. All trading decisions are yours alone. Past signal accuracy does not guarantee future results. Trade at your own risk.
What markets does Vaxelo work on? +
Vaxelo V1.0 works on all markets on Kalshi. Polymarket support is coming in V2.0. The extension detects when you are on a Kalshi market page and activates automatically.
How do I upgrade to unlimited signals? +
Click the Vaxelo extension icon in Chrome, go to the Setup tab, and click Upgrade. You will be taken to our Lemon Squeezy checkout. After purchase you will receive a license key by email — paste it in the Setup tab to unlock unlimited signals immediately.
