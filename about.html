The story behind Vaxelo — and why giving retail traders institutional tools matters.

The Story
"I was betting on Kalshi with my gut and losing money. I needed to build something smarter — so I did."
— Dawit Debela, Founder of Vaxelo AI

Dawit Debela is a 33-year-old builder from Richardson, Texas. He started trading on Kalshi like most retail traders — following comments, trusting gut feelings, and watching money disappear.

He noticed something: the people winning consistently weren't smarter. They had better information. They were checking multiple sources, comparing prices across platforms, and sizing their bets mathematically. Things that took hours to do manually.

So he built Vaxelo AI — a Chrome extension that does all of that in 10 seconds. 4 AI agents fire in parallel, analyze everything a smart trader would check, and output one clear signal.

He built it solo. He tests it with his own money. Every signal Vaxelo gives, Dawit has placed a real bet on. That is not marketing. That is skin in the game.
