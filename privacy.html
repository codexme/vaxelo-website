Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

The short version: Vaxelo AI does not collect or store any personal information on our servers. All your data stays on your own device. We never see it.

1. Who We Are

Vaxelo AI is a Chrome extension that analyzes prediction markets on Kalshi and provides AI-powered trading signals. Operated by the Vaxelo AI team. Contact: hello@vaxelo.xyz

2. What Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number
  • Your location or IP address
  • Your Kalshi login credentials or account password
  • Your bank account or credit card information
  • Any browsing history outside of Kalshi market pages

3. What Data Vaxelo Does Read

When you visit a Kalshi market page, Vaxelo reads the following publicly visible data from that page to generate your signal:

  • Market prices and odds displayed on the page
  • Orderbook activity — bet amounts and sides (YES/NO) visible to all visitors
  • Public comments posted on the market page
  • Market title, category, and time remaining

This data is processed on your device and sent to Groq AI using your own personal API key to generate a signal. It is never sent to or stored on Vaxelo servers.

4. What Is Stored Locally on Your Device

The following is stored only on your device using Chrome local storage. We cannot access it:

  • Your Groq API key — entered by you, never transmitted to Vaxelo
  • Your prediction history and signal results
  • Your extension settings and preferences
  • Your subscription license key — verified directly with Lemon Squeezy, not stored on our servers

5. Third-Party Services

  • Groq AI — Processes market data using your own API key to generate signals. See groq.com/privacy.
  • Lemon Squeezy — Handles subscription payments securely. We never see your card details. See lemonsqueezy.com/privacy.
  • Kalshi — We read publicly visible market data from pages you visit. We do not log into or access your Kalshi account.

6. Payments

All payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy. We do not store or have access to your credit card or payment information at any time.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Vaxelo AI does not use cookies. We do not use Google Analytics or any tracking tools. We do not track your behavior across websites.

8. Data Security

Because we do not collect your personal data on our servers, there is no central database that could be breached. All data stays on your local device under Chrome's security model.

9. Children's Privacy

Vaxelo AI is intended for users 18 years and older only. Prediction market trading requires users to be of legal age in their jurisdiction.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The date at the top will always reflect the most recent version.
